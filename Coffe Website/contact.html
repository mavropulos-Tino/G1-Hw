FAQ

What is the difference between espresso and regular coffee?

Espresso uses pressure to brew quickly; regular coffee brews slowly with gravity or immersion.

Which coffee roast is strongest?

Dark roasts taste stronger, but light roasts actually have slightly more caffeine.

How should I store my coffee beans?

Keep them in an airtight container away from heat, light, and moisture.

How long do coffee beans stay fresh?

Whole beans stay fresh for about 2–4 weeks after opening; ground coffee loses freshness faster.

What grind size should I use?

Espresso: fine — Pour-over: medium — French press: coarse.
